Simply Know About

HxCo, “the Healthcare Collective”, is a platform for all stake-holders to join hands to build healthy communities.

The last 2 decades has seen lots of advances in Healthcare delivery – finding cures for conditions that, even 10 years ago, were considered uncurable. However, this presents a problem of its own – people now live longer, putting immense pressure on healthcare system. The growth in healthcare resources, while significant, hasn’t kept pace – and the gap is only likely to increase over time. The only way out is to build healthy communities – and focus on reducing overall healthcare burden.

The solution to a healthy society has several dimensions – at the core is an aware patient. Institutional payers, viz. insurance companies and the government, gain most by building healthy communities and have to play a prominent role in developing the solution. They will be supported by trusted, capable, providers who subscribe to the philosophy. Technology can be a game changer – bringing healthcare to the most remote communities, at affordable costs and high quality.

HxCo will bring together the different elements and help move the needle from “Sickcare” to “Healthcare”.
